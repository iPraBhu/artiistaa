## Timeless Elegance for Your Dining Space

Transform your dining table with this exquisite handwoven cotton table runner, featuring traditional block print patterns that showcase the rich textile heritage of Rajasthan. Each piece is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who have mastered the ancient art of hand weaving and block printing.

### Traditional Craftsmanship

This table runner is created using traditional pit looms, where skilled weavers work with precision to create the perfect texture and weave. The cotton threads are carefully selected for their quality and strength, ensuring a durable yet soft fabric that improves with age.

### Block Printing Artistry

The beautiful patterns are created using hand-carved wooden blocks, a technique that has been practiced in Rajasthan for centuries. Each block is carefully aligned and pressed onto the fabric using natural dyes, creating intricate geometric and floral motifs that are both contemporary and timeless.

### Care Instructions

To maintain the beauty and longevity of your table runner:
- Hand wash in cold water with mild, natural detergent
- Avoid bleach or harsh chemicals
- Line dry in shade to preserve colors
- Iron on medium heat if needed
- Store folded in a cool, dry place

### Styling Tips

- Layer over a solid tablecloth for added texture
- Pair with matching placemats and napkins
- Combine with brass or copper accessories for an authentic Indian look
- Use as a base for candles, flowers, or decorative bowls

### About the Artisan Community

This table runner is created by the women's cooperative in Sanganer, Rajasthan, known for their exceptional block printing skills. The cooperative provides fair wages and flexible working hours, allowing women to balance traditional craft work with family responsibilities.
