## Traditional Craftsmanship Meets Festive Beauty

This exquisite hand-painted clay diya represents the perfect fusion of traditional Indian artistry and functional design. Crafted by skilled potters from Gujarat, each diya is individually shaped, fired, and painted with intricate floral motifs that celebrate India's rich cultural heritage.

### The Art of Diya Making

Our artisan partners have been creating these beautiful diyas for generations, using techniques passed down through their families. The process begins with locally sourced clay, carefully molded by hand on traditional pottery wheels. After the initial shaping, each diya is fired in wood-fired kilns, giving it strength and durability.

### Hand-painted Excellence

What makes these diyas truly special is the detailed hand-painting process. Using natural pigments and fine brushes, our artists create delicate floral patterns inspired by traditional Indian motifs. No two diyas are exactly alike, making each piece a unique work of art.

### Eco-Friendly Choice

Made from natural clay and painted with eco-friendly colors, these diyas are completely biodegradable and safe for both indoor and outdoor use. By choosing handmade pottery, you're supporting sustainable crafts and traditional artisan livelihoods.

### Care Instructions

To maintain the beauty of your hand-painted diya:
- Clean gently with a dry cloth
- Avoid soaking in water
- Store in a dry place when not in use
- Handle with care as it's made of natural clay

### About the Artisan

This diya is crafted by Master Potter Ramesh Prajapati from Kutch, Gujarat, who has been creating pottery for over 30 years. His workshop employs 15 local artisans, including women from the community, providing sustainable income and preserving traditional skills.
