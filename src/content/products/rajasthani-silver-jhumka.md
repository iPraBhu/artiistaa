## About Rajasthani Jhumkas

Jhumkas are iconic to Indian jewelry heritage:

- **Ancient Origins**: 2000+ years old, mentioned in Sanskrit texts
- **Royal Heritage**: Worn by Rajput queens and princesses
- **Cultural Significance**: Symbol of femininity and marital bliss
- **Regional Variations**: Each state has unique design elements
- **Spiritual Meaning**: Bell shape resonates positive energy
