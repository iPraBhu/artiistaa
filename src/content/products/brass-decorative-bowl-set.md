## Timeless Brass Artistry

This stunning set of three brass bowls showcases the exceptional metalworking skills of Moradabad artisans, known as the "Brass City" of India. Each bowl is individually shaped, engraved, and polished to create functional art pieces that bring warmth and elegance to any home.

### Master Craftsmanship

Our skilled metalworkers use traditional techniques passed down through generations:
- **Hand Hammering**: Each bowl is shaped from a single sheet of brass using traditional hammers
- **Engraving**: Intricate patterns are hand-engraved using specialized tools
- **Polishing**: Multiple stages of polishing create the beautiful golden finish

### Care & Maintenance

Brass naturally develops a patina over time, which many consider part of its beauty. To maintain the golden shine:
- Clean regularly with brass polish or a mixture of lemon juice and salt
- Wipe dry immediately after cleaning
- Store in a dry place to prevent tarnishing
- Avoid dishwasher use - hand wash only

### About Moradabad Brassware

Moradabad has been the center of brass handicrafts in India for over 400 years. The artisans here are renowned for their skill in creating both functional and decorative brass items. This craft provides livelihoods for thousands of families and keeps ancient techniques alive.
